Let \(\mathcal{S} = \{\varphi \in \Phi : \mathbb{P}_{\Phi}(\varphi) > 0\}\) be the support of the prior.
Theorem 3.1 (Posterior consistency). [4]
Under some assumptions, there exists \(\varphi^* \in \mathcal{S}\) such that for almost every \((y, x)\), \[ \pi(y \mid x, \mathcal{D}_n) \xrightarrow[n \to \infty]{} \varphi^*(y \mid x) \quad \text{a.s.} \] and \(\varphi^*\) is the KL-optimal approximation of \(p_0\) in \(\mathcal{S}\): \(\quad \varphi^* = \arg\min_{\varphi \in \mathcal{S}} \text{KL}(\varphi \parallel p_0)\).
