TabPFN

Recepie
  • Generate millions of synthetic datasets;
  • Pre-train a transformer;
  • Approximate the PPD and predicts on a new dataset in one forward pass;
  • Beat the strongest AutoML baselines in seconds rather than hours.
Limitations
  • Focuses on datasets with \(n \le 10\text{k}\), \(\le 100\) numerical features and \(\le 10\) classes; beyond these limits baselines still win.
  • Memory scales quadratically with n;
  • Supports only classification, without regression.
  • Only approximates causal reasoning over a distribution of SCMs, without explicitly performing it.
  • Only IID samples.
