Features' Statistical Properties

-> Columns with similar skewness (resp. kurtosis) tend to cluster together
-> This suggests that TFcol encodes distributional properties in a structured manner, and cells in a column are probably embedded to reflect their statistical role (e.g., min, max, mean, mode).
-> Therefore, the learned feature embeddings should distinguish features based on their unique distributional properties, effectively serving as feature identifiers.
