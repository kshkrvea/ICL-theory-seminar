TabPFN for SBI

NPE-PFN — TabPFN is the Posterior
Inputs\(\mathcal{D},\ x_o\)
→
❄TabPFN\(\theta^{j}_k\sim p(\theta^{j}\mid \theta^{<j}_k,x_o,\mathcal{D})\)

\(\theta^{1}_k\!\to\!\theta^{2}_k\!\to\cdots\to\theta^{d}_k\)
⇒
Posterior samples \(\theta_k = (\theta^{1}_k, \theta^{2}_k, \cdots, \theta^{d}_k)\)
PFN-NPE — TabPFN as a Summary Network
Inputs\(\mathcal{D},\ x_o\)
→
🔥Approximation\(q_\phi(\theta \mid h(x))\)
▲
🔥Summary network\(h_\psi(x)\)
❄TabPFN\(h(x)\)frozen · emb. + PCA
⇒
Posterior\(q_\phi(\theta \mid x_o)\)
Two ways to bring TabPFN into SBI, both against the classical pipeline (slide 1).
UPPER — NPE-PFN (Vetter et al. 2025): the radical version — TabPFN replaces the ENTIRE posterior estimator. Format the simulated pairs as a tabular regression context (features = x, target = θ), feed x_o, and read off the posterior autoregressively: θ¹, then θ² | θ¹, … — each coordinate conditioned on the earlier ones, x_o, and D. No per-task training; it's TabPFN's in-context forward pass.
LOWER — PFN-NPE (Pickens et al. 2026): the minimal version — keep the classical stage-(ii) pipeline and swap ONLY the summary network: freeze a pretrained TabPFN and use its final-layer embeddings (parameter-wise context + PCA→64) as the representation h(x). The trained density head q_φ(θ|h) is unchanged. That single swap is the method.
~1.5 min each: contrast "TabPFN IS the estimator" vs "TabPFN is just the frozen summary".

PFN-NPE — Results

-> No single winner — NPE-PFN leads many standard tasks, PFN-NPE wins mixture / nuisance settings.
Takeaway is task-dependent, not "PFN wins":
- NPE-PFN (autoregressive) has the lowest joint C2ST on many standard benchmark tasks.
- PFN-NPE (frozen summary + flow) is strongest on mixture and nuisance-coordinate settings, and beats the learned-summary NPE baseline on most tasks.
- Both PFN approaches still struggle on hard joint-structure tasks (SLCP, Lotka–Volterra): frozen summaries keep good marginals but lose some dependence structure.
Framing: this is a supplement — the point is that a tabular foundation model plugs cleanly into SBI two different ways. Read the papers for the details.
